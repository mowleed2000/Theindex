Correction Requests

For business representatives requesting a classification review.

corrections@aivisibilityindex.org

Press & Media

For journalists and media enquiries about the index.

press@aivisibilityindex.org

Response Times

  • General enquiries: Within 10 business days
  • Correction requests: Acknowledgement within 5 business days
  • Press enquiries: Within 3 business days

Response times may vary during peak periods.

Enquiries We Do Not Respond To

The following types of enquiries will not receive a response:

  • • Requests for consulting, advisory, or optimisation services
  • • Requests to remove a business from the index
  • • Requests to expedite assessments
  • • Commercial partnership proposals
  • • Sales or marketing communications

The index does not provide paid services of any kind. We do not offer advice on how to improve classifications.
