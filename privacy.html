Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

The Local Business AI Visibility Index ("the Index") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to this website. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Website Visitors

This website does not collect personal information from visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services that identify individual users.

Businesses Listed in the Index

The Index contains publicly available business information only, including:

  • Business names
  • Business sectors
  • Visibility classifications based on public signals

No private or confidential business information is collected or published.

Data Sources

All assessments are conducted using publicly accessible information, including:

  • Public websites and web pages
  • Public business directories
  • Publicly available structured data

We do not access private systems, customer databases, or confidential business records.

Correction Requests

If you are a business representative and believe your listing contains inaccurate information, you may submit a correction request via email. Correction requests should include:

  • Business name as listed
  • Nature of the inaccuracy
  • Supporting evidence if applicable

Email: corrections@aivisibilityindex.org

Third Parties

This website does not share visitor information with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade any data.

External Links

This website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact:

enquiries@aivisibilityindex.org
