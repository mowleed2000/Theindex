Correction Requests

If a business representative believes that a classification no longer reflects current public signals, a correction review may be requested.

Correction reviews are conducted to determine whether observable public signals have changed since the last assessment. They do not consider factors outside the scope of the established methodology.

Submission of a correction request does not guarantee reclassification. All reviews are conducted according to standard methodology.

Eligibility

Correction requests may be submitted under the following conditions:

  • The business is currently listed in the index
  • The request is submitted by an authorised representative of the business
  • At least 30 days have passed since the last assessment
  • The request concerns publicly observable signals within scope of assessment

What Can Be Reviewed

Correction reviews assess changes in:

  • • Consistency of public business information
  • • Clarity of publicly accessible service descriptions
  • • Machine-readable location signals
  • • Redundancy across public platforms
  • • Coherence of third-party references

What Cannot Be Reviewed

The following are outside the scope of correction reviews:

  • • Quality of service or products
  • • Customer reviews or reputation
  • • Regulatory compliance or certifications
  • • Private or proprietary information

Submission Process

Correction requests should be submitted by email with the following information:

01

Business Details

Business name exactly as listed in the index, region, and sector.

02

Representative Information

Name and role of the person submitting the request, with verifiable connection to the business.

03

Nature of Request

Brief description of what has changed since the last assessment.

04

Public References

URLs or references to publicly accessible sources supporting the request (optional but recommended).

Submission Address

corrections@aivisibilityindex.org

Review Process

Upon receipt of a valid correction request:

  • Acknowledgement: Requests are acknowledged within 5 business days
  • Assessment: A new assessment is conducted using current public signals
  • Decision: Classification is confirmed or updated based on findings
  • Notification: Requestor is informed of the outcome within 30 days

All decisions are final. There is no appeals process.

Important Notes

The index does not offer consulting, advisory, or optimisation services. We do not provide guidance on how to improve classifications. Correction requests that seek such guidance will not receive a response.

The correction process exists solely to ensure that classifications reflect current publicly observable signals. It is not a commercial service.

This index reflects an independent assessment of publicly available information. It does not constitute endorsement, certification, or guarantee of business quality or service delivery.
