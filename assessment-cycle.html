Assessment Cycle

Frequency, timing, and schedule of index assessments.

Assessment Schedule

Businesses in the index are assessed on a rolling basis. Each assessment captures the state of publicly observable signals at a specific point in time.

Classifications do not reflect real-time status and may not reflect changes that occur after the assessment date.

Assessment Frequency

  • Standard cycle: Monthly assessments for all listed businesses
  • New additions: Assessed upon inclusion in the index
  • Correction reviews: Assessed upon valid request (minimum 30 days since last assessment)
  • Region additions: Assessed upon region inclusion

Assessment dates for individual businesses are displayed in the business directory and region pages. The "Assessed" field indicates when the most recent assessment was conducted.

Assessment Timing

Assessments are conducted throughout each calendar month. There is no specific order in which businesses are assessed within a cycle.

Factors that do not influence assessment timing:

  • • Current classification
  • • Business size or type
  • • Previous assessment results
  • • External requests or communications

Index Updates

Index updates are published upon completion of assessment cycles. This includes:

  • Updated classifications for existing businesses
  • New business additions
  • New region additions (when applicable)
  • Removal of businesses no longer operating (when identified)

The index version number is updated when significant structural changes are made.

Historical Data

Historical assessment data may be archived in future versions of the index. Currently, only the most recent assessment is displayed for each business.

Archival features under consideration:

  • • Classification change history
  • • Monthly snapshot archives
  • • Aggregate trend reporting

This index reflects an independent assessment of publicly available information. It does not constitute endorsement, certification, or guarantee of business quality or service delivery.
