About This Index

The Local Business AI Visibility Index is an independent research initiative that monitors how local businesses are represented to AI-driven discovery systems. It operates without commercial relationships with any listed business.

Independence

No business can pay to be listed, removed, or reclassified. All assessments are conducted using publicly available information only.

Assessment Cycle

Businesses are reviewed on a rolling basis. Each entry reflects signals observed at the time of assessment, not real-time status.

Scope

This index currently covers local service businesses operating in the United Kingdom. Geographic expansion is under consideration.

Classification Framework

Businesses are assigned one of three classifications based on the interpretability of their public digital signals by automated systems.

Classification reflects interpretability of public signals by automated systems. It is not a measure of quality, reputation, or compliance.

Clear

Public signals allow consistent automated interpretation. The business is reliably identifiable and categorisable by AI systems.

Ambiguous

Signals exist but introduce uncertainty or misclassification risk. The business may be inconsistently represented across platforms.

Uninterpretable

Signals are insufficient for reliable automated interpretation. The business may be overlooked or misrepresented by AI systems.
