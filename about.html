About This Index

Purpose, scope, and operational principles of the Local Business AI Visibility Index.

Purpose

The Local Business AI Visibility Index documents how AI-driven discovery systems interpret local businesses based on publicly observable digital signals.

As automated systems increasingly mediate how consumers discover local services, the interpretability of business information to these systems becomes relevant. This index provides a reference point for understanding that interpretability.

The index does not offer services, sell products, or provide recommendations. It exists as a public reference document.

Operational Principles

Independence

The index operates without commercial relationships with any listed business. No business can pay to be listed, removed, or reclassified.

Transparency

Assessment methodology is publicly documented. Classification criteria are applied consistently across all assessed businesses.

Neutrality

The index does not advocate for any particular outcome. It documents observable signal interpretability without judgement.

Current Scope

The index currently covers local service businesses operating in selected regions of the United Kingdom.

  • Geographic coverage: Luton, Brentford (additional regions under consideration)
  • Business types: Local service businesses with physical premises or defined service areas
  • Sectors assessed: Barbershops, Cafés, Dentists, Pubs, Restaurants, Trade Services
  • Assessment frequency: Rolling assessments conducted on a monthly cycle

What This Index Is Not

To maintain clarity about the nature of this publication:

  • Not a rating service: We do not rate business quality, customer service, or value.
  • Not a marketing tool: We do not promote businesses or provide advertising services.
  • Not a consultancy: We do not offer advice or services to improve classifications.
  • Not a directory: We do not provide business contact information or facilitate transactions.
  • Not a certification: We do not endorse, certify, or guarantee any business.
